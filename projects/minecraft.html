Minecraft Projects

Here you'll find all of my projects on Minecraft. 
Each has a link towards the source code (if it is the case) on GitHub, the API documentation, a presentation video clip, and from where the plugin may be purchased.

ARCADE GAMES

The Arcade Games offers you a wide range of fun and casual minigames which are played in a fast-paced style.


GRINCH SIMULATOR

Grinch Simulator is a brand new game inspired by parkour and collection. You play as a Grinch – an evil Christmas creature whose only desire is to ruin the holiday for everyone.

COPS AND CRIMS

Cops and Crims is based off the popular eSports shooter Counter Strike: Global Offensive. Using a custom resource pack, both teams arm themselves with guns, pistols, and even grenades.

BEDWARS

In Bed Wars, you and up to 4 teammates spawn on islands. You must gather 4 resources: Iron, Gold, Diamonds and Emeralds to buy items and upgrades at the Shopkeepers on your island.

Each island has a Bed - if your Bed is destroyed you can no longer respawn. Place blocks around your bed to defend it from being destroyed. Destroy other teams' beds then kill them to win!


THE LAB

The Laboratory is a game ran by our local science expert, Dr Zuk! And he needs your help! Zuk has been working on many awesome new exciting inventions, but they're untested. Are you up for the job?

On entering TheLab, you will be debriefed on the experiments that Dr.Zuk wants you to perform. Each game will see you play a random 3 of 14 games.
